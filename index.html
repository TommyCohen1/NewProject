The fifth-generation Honda Civic is an automobile produced by Honda from 1991 until 1995. It debuted in Japan on September 9, 1991. At its introduction, it won the Car of the Year Japan award for the second time.

Body Style Coupe

1995 Civic ESi Coupe trims are introduced for the 1993 model year,There were 2 models, the DX (EJ2),and EX-S (EJ1)

The coupe version built in both Canada and the United States, was also exported to European and Japanese markets.

In 1992-1995 years Honda produced similar models to the coupe such as Sedan(4doors) and Hatchback. Those are much less sport looking than the Coupe.
